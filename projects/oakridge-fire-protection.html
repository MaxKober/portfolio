Oakridge Centre — Fire Protection

Mixed-Use • Design-Build / Stipulated Price • Vancouver, BC

Design-Build Role: Project Manager — Fire Protection 50+ Packages Status: Completed

Overview

Client: Private developer (Oakridge redevelopment)

Delivery of a comprehensive fire protection program across a large mixed-use redevelopment, including virtual construction design coordination, procurement and commissioning of sprinkler and life-safety systems. The work integrated with multiple packages and BIM/VDC workflows.

Scope of Work

  • Integrated fire systems into BIM coordination to avoid clashes with other building elements and services.
  • Managed procurement, factory acceptance and on-site delivery of equipment and pipework across 50+ packages.
  • Coordinated testing, commissioning and handover with AHJ and commissioning authorities.
  • Supported close-out documentation and as-built record preparation.

Challenge

Complex phasing required simultaneous construction and fit-out across mixed uses while maintaining existing retail operations nearby.

Solution

Phased delivery plan, detailed logistics and BIM clash resolution ensured packages were sequenced for minimal disruption and timely handover.

Result

Systems delivered with minimal schedule slippage; successful AHJ inspections and smooth commissioning of wet and dry systems.
